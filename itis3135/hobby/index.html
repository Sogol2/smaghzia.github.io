What is Dancing?

Dancing is the art of expressing stories and feelings through rhythm, music, and body movement. It connects cultures, improves coordination, and spreads joy.

Styles I love:

  • Hip-hop — energetic, creative, and bold
  • Contemporary — emotional and free-flowing
  • Latin — full of rhythm and partnership

Who Am I

Hi, I’m Sogol! Dancing has been part of my life since childhood. It’s my creative outlet and the way I recharge after studying. I dance whenever I need energy or peace of mind.

“When words fail, movement speaks.”

When I Dance

I usually dance in the evenings to relax and let go of stress. On weekends, I spend more time learning choreography or freestyle with friends.

Where I Dance

Anywhere music plays! My favorite spots are the campus dance studio, my living room, or open parks with speakers and friends.

Dancing outside during sunset feels magical—the world becomes your stage.

How I Practice

I start with a warm-up, then focus on one move or combo at a time. Recording sessions helps me notice posture and timing. I also learn by watching professionals and repeating small sequences.

  1. Stretch for 10 minutes
  2. Pick one song and focus on rhythm
  3. Freestyle for confidence

Why I Love Dancing

Dancing gives me freedom, confidence, and happiness. It helps me express what words can’t and connect with others who share the same rhythm.

  • Emotion: transforms stress into energy
  • Fitness: keeps me active and strong
  • Community: dancing brings people together
